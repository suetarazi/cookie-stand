Salmon Cookies are a delicious, high protein treat! We are a family of fisherman who have enjoyed salmon fishing for years. We have perfected the perfect salmon cookie based on our grandmother's recipe. Years ago, she used to bake a batch of these and send them off with us on our fishing expeditions.

We have improved upon her recipe by adding chocolate chips and making them dairy-free. They are full of protein (18 grams per cookie) and are rich in Omega-3 acids that are good for the brain.

They are delicious with a cup of coffee or as a nutritious snack by themselves. Enjoy your cookies from any one of our 5 worldwide locations listed below.

We also do catering for special events and can ship to your location, so call for more information.
